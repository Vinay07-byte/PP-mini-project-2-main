Eye-Opening Environmental Facts

Global Warming Accelerating

The Earth has warmed by 1.1°C since pre-industrial times. We're heading towards 1.5°C within 20 years if emissions continue.

Plastic in Oceans

8 million tons of plastic enter our oceans every year. By 2050, plastic will outweigh fish in the sea.

Deforestation Crisis

27 soccer fields worth of forest are lost every minute. We lose 10 billion trees annually.

Food Waste Impact

1.3 billion tons of food is wasted yearly - equal to 1/3 of all food produced. This creates 8-10% of global emissions.

Water Crisis

By 2025, half the world's population will live in water-stressed areas. A single event can waste thousands of liters.

Recycling Impact

Recycling just half of household waste saves 2,400 pounds of CO2 per household annually. Proper waste management matters!
