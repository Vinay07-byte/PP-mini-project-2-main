About EcoSense

What is EcoSense?

EcoSense is a sustainability-focused platform designed to help individuals and event planners understand and reduce the environmental impact of their catering choices. The website calculates the carbon footprint generated by food, resources, and waste during events, giving users clear insights into how their decisions affect the planet. Along with footprint analysis, EcoSense also connects users with nearby waste management and recycling organizations, making it easier to responsibly dispose of event waste for proper treatment and reuse. By combining smart calculation with practical local solutions, EcoSense empowers users to plan smarter, greener, and more responsible events.

Our Mission

To raise sustainability and waste management awareness with AI scanner, proper disposal guidance, and recycling center connections.

Our Vision

A world where every event and individual choice contributes positively to environmental sustainability and responsible resource management.
