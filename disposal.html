Proper Waste Disposal Guide

Learn correct disposal methods for different waste types. Scan items first!

Plastic Waste

DO

  • Rinse clean
  • Remove caps
  • Recycle ♻️

DON'T

  • Throw in landfill
  • Burn plastics
  • Mix with organic

Glass Waste

DO

  • Remove metal lids
  • Rinse clean
  • Glass bin ♻️

DON'T

  • Break before disposal
  • Mix with plastic
  • Window/mirror glass

Metal Waste

DO

  • Rinse food residue
  • Flatten cans
  • Metal recycling ♻️

DON'T

  • Aerosol cans with gas
  • Motor oil containers
  • Mix with paper

E-Waste (Electronics)

DO

  • E-waste centers ONLY
  • Tape cable ends
  • Certified recyclers

DON'T

  • Landfill/regular trash
  • Burn electronics
  • Donate broken items

Organic Waste

DO

  • Compost food waste
  • Garden waste compost
  • Vermicomposting

DON'T

  • Mix with recyclables
  • Plastic-lined waste
  • Landfill organic
